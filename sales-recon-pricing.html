Pricing Factors

Price Multipliers

1. Avg Contract Value (ACV)

Rationale

Larger ACV means each additional qualified meeting / pipeline opportunity has greater revenue impact; customers with higher ACV will have higher willingness to pay.

Price Multiplier

  • < $50k 1x
  • $50k – $100k 1.1x
  • $100 – $250k 1.15x
  • > $250k 1.25x

2. Geographic Footprint

Rationale

Multi-region/global teams benefit more from SalesRecon's ability to coordinate timing, localize briefs, and preserve institutional knowledge across regions.

Price Multiplier

  • Multiregional 1.1x
  • Single region 1x
