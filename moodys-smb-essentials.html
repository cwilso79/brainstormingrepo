How the Model Works

Our transparent pricing structure is designed to scale with your business while keeping costs predictable.

  • Access Package: Entry tier provides full platform access with all essential features included
  • Level 1 Usage Allowance: Annual commitment ranging from $10k–$49k based on your entity volume
  • Unified Consumption: All consumption—whether via UI, API, or MCP—draws from the same usage allowance
  • Step‑Up Only Model: Designed to grow with you; upgrade seamlessly as your compliance needs expand
